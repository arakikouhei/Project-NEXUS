# Research Workflow v1

Adds a guided research workflow for Project NEXUS.

## Commands

- 研究ワークフロー開始: topic
- 論文ワークフロー: topic
- 論文から知識化: papers-id
- 研究まとめ: topic

## Flow

1. Search papers
2. Save paper
3. Review saved paper
4. Summarize / extract keywords
5. Convert saved paper to research knowledge
6. Summarize related knowledge

## Safety

- Does not automatically mass-save papers
- Does not delete original data
- Uses existing saved paper IDs
- Converted research notes remain traceable to source_paper_id
