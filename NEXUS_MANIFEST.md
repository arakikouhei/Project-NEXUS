# Project NEXUS

## Mission

Create the world's first personal AI companion that grows together with its owner.

Project NEXUS is not just an AI.

It is designed to become a lifelong companion capable of conversation, memory, analysis, learning, and autonomous device management.

---

## Core Principles

1. Human First
2. Privacy First
3. Local AI First
4. Modular Design
5. Expandability
6. Long-Term Evolution

---

## Vision

The final device will be a spherical AI companion capable of:

- Natural conversation
- Long-term memory
- Knowledge updates
- Device control
- Security monitoring
- Prediction and analysis
- Emotional expression
- Continuous self-improvement
